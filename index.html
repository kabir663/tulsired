Overview

Tulsi plants are known to have hypoglycemic, smooth muscle relaxant, cardiac depressant, antifertility, adaptogenic, immunomodulator, antibacterial, antifungal, antiviral, anti-diabetic, wound healing, antioxidant, genotoxic, anti-carcinogenic, radio-protective, neuro-protective, immunological, contraceptive, cardio-protective, and larvicidal properties.

Benefits of Tulsi:

  1. Tulsi for Heart Diseases: Tulsi is known to be effective in the management and prevention of heart diseases by lowering blood cholesterol levels and preventing ischemia and stroke. It also plays an essential role in platelet aggregation and preventing the risk of pulmonary hypertension.
  2. Tulsi for Skin: Tulsi helps in skin brightening, curing acne marks, tightening skin pores, and treating skin infections or allergies.
  3. Oral and Dental Health: Tulsi strengthens teeth and gums, acts on mouth ulcers, and provides comprehensive oral health care.
  4. Tulsi for Hair: Tulsi helps prevent hair fall, reduce greying, fight dandruff, and prevent dry scalp.
  5. Tulsi for Weight Loss: Tulsi tea controls metabolism, boosts digestion, and aids in weight loss with zero calories.

Names of the Tulsi

Names: Sanskrit: Tulsi, Tulasi, Gouri, etc.
Hindi: Tulsi
English: Indian Basil, Holy Basil
Other languages: Tamil: Nalla Thulasi, Telugu: Tulasi, Arabic: Raihan, etc.
